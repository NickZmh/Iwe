A strong network for the 8% who don’t have one

In Denmark, 8% of every generation don’t get a job or continue in education after lower secondary school. You can help change this by giving the 14- to 16-year-olds a strong network and the help, support and recognition that come with it. Join iwe to change their outlook for good.
